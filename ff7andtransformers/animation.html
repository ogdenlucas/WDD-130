Hop on board! The legend becomes real as we launch the first phase of the most amazing animated rock and roll adventure in the world! The original animated series known as The Transformers merges with the legendary group of heroes known as AVALANCHE from the incredible Final Fantasy VII series of media in this exciting adventure that will keep you on the edge of your seats! Ride with us as the Autobots, together with the forces of the WRO, or World Regenesis Organization, and the mercenary group known as AVALANCHE, fight for the freedom of the whole human race against the evil Decepticons and their allies, the Sons of Jenova! It's a world where the unexpected merges with the divine, where the mighty, the mad, and the magical will have their final battle, and where the forces of darkness, while they may seem powerful, will never be allowed to bask in the light of the forces of good! It's a world beyond your experience! It's a world beyond your imagination!

The characters in this show and movie will engage in battles against each other, which means the good characters will fight the evil characters. The battles will be exciting and tense, but the scenes will not have gratuitous violence.

This show and movie will include animation that is realistic, but not so much so that the audience will suddenly get nervous. As such, the animation style will be reminiscent of the 1980s.
