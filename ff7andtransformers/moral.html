This series and movie will be educational because it will subtly teach proper morals, but kids will just see it as a fun and exciting source of entertainment. This will be a help to families by eliminating bad language and showing the importance of honesty, kindness, and integrity.

Good moral values include honesty, kindness, integrity, courage, commitment, faithfulness, loyalty, respect, hard work, compassion, gratitude, sharing, responsibility, cooperation, and generosity.

Honesty can be tied to integrity and loyalty. Honest people do not lie, cheat, or steal.

Sometimes it takes courage to be kind. Kindness can also earn you the respect of others. It is important to make a commitment to respect other people.
