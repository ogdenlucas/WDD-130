Our children need entertainment that is exciting, but at the same time can be worthwhile to watch, is wholesome and family friendly, and teaches them good moral values while also having a positive effect on them. The children will celebrate when a battle is won by a good character, but cry when a good character they love dies. That is what this show needs to feel like.
